Boutaina ETTETUANI
Research Team in Data and Systems Engineering, National School of Applied Sciences, Abdelmalek Essaadi University, Tangier, MOROCCO

Boutaina ETTETUANI was born in Tangier, Morocco in December 1992. Currently a PhD student in bioinformatics whose subject is a meta-analysis of next generation sequencing data related to glomerulopathies for the search of therapeutic targets involved in the activation of the C3 protein. Graduate of a Master's degree in Bioinformatics and Complexs Systems at the National School of Applied Sciences of Tangier (ENSAT). The university curriculum includes a Bachelor's Degree in computer science from the Faculty of Science and Technology of Tangier (FSTT) and a baccalaureate in physics and chemistry. b.ettetuani@uae.ac.ma,ettetuani.boutaina@gmail.com

Ahmed MOUSSA
Systems and Data Enginneering Team, National School of Applied Sciences, Abdelmalek Essaadi University,Tangier, MOROCCO

Ahmed Moussa received the MS degree in Automatic Control from the University of Oujda, Morocco, in 1994, and the Ph.D. in Data analysis and Information Theory from the University of Kenitra, Morocco, in 2001. He worked two years as a post-graduate researcher at the University of Lille-France, and Three Years as Bioinformatician at Sanofi-Aventis Research Group in Montpellier-France. Between 2006 an 2010 he was assistant Professor at the Abdelmalek Essaadi University of Tangier Morocco. And from 2010 to 2016 associate Professor. Since 2017, he is Full Professor in Bioinformatics and Data Analysis, and the Director of the National School of Applied Sciences of Tangier-Morocco amoussa@uae.ac.ma,ahmed.moussa2@gmail.com
