Genes Interaction

As the number of interacting genes increases, and because the traditional statistical methods are limited to their ability to identify interacting genes in high dimensions data. We developed a novel model called mixed-gene tissues interaction (MGTI) for identifying these regulators using high-throughput omics data.

Figure1: Genetic interactions of omics data generally refer to a combination pairs of genes into different tissues of fixed clusters, whose contribution to a phenotype between specific variants in complex traits and tissues remaining a major challenge, emerging statistical methods explained by their independent effects, for mapping complex genotype-phenotype information

  • It first calculated gene prioritization model based matrix-Expression-Similarity-Frequency, and then identifies significantly interacted mixed-gene in all selected tissues using a MGTI model, represented as interaction score (formula. 4), which was substantiated to reflect strengths of regulatory interactions within each mixed gene block.

    • Sp represents a score of genes prioritization.

    • The interaction scores are between [0,1].

    • When the interaction scores are ∼= [0.6 , 1], MGTI model reflect an increased regulation of diseases.

    • When the interaction scores are ∼= [0.1 , 0.5], MGTI model reflect a decreased regulation of diseases, in which we should interact more with other possible genes.

  • As a result, identifying significant interactions between prioritized genes results, understanding of etiology and improving the prediction and ability of linear prioritized genes.
